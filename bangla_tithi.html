1. Introduction

Welcome to Bangla Tithi Darpan ("we", "our", or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Bengali calendar and tithi application.

2. Information We Collect

üìç Approximate Location (Optional)

We may request access to your approximate location solely to calculate accurate sunrise and sunset times for your region. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts or social media accounts
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics

3. How We Use Information

  • Location data: Used only to display accurate sunrise/sunset times based on your geographic area
  • Calendar preferences: Stored locally on your device for personalization
  • Reminder settings: Stored locally to send tithi notifications

4. Data Storage

All user preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using standard storage mechanisms. We do not maintain any user databases or cloud storage for personal information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may fetch calendar data from Supabase, a cloud database service, to provide up-to-date tithi information. This connection only retrieves public calendar data and does not transmit any personal information.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with any third parties.

Your information stays on your device. Period.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny location permission (sunrise times will use default location)
  • Clear app data through your device settings
  • Request deletion of any data by contacting us
  • Uninstall the app at any time

9. Security

All network communications use HTTPS encryption. Since we don't collect personal data, there is minimal risk of data breach.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
